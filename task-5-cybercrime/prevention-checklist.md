# Stay Safe Online

## Prevention Checklist
1. Do not click on suspicious links  
2. Verify sender email addresses  
3. Use strong and unique passwords  
4. Enable two-factor authentication  
5. Never share OTP or bank details  
6. Use secure websites (https)  
7. Regularly update passwords  
8. Avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions

---

## Financial/UPI SAFETY:
1. Double-check UPI IDs before payment  
2. Never approve unknown payment requests
