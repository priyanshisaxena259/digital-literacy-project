# Social Media Checklist
## DO’s :
1. Keep profiles professional  
2. Use strong passwords  
3. Think before posting  
4. Verify information before sharing  
5. Respect others online  

---

## DON’Ts :
1. Share personal information publicly  
2. Use offensive language  
3. Click on unknown links  
4. Spread misinformation  
5. Engage in cyberbullying
